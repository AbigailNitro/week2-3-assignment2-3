Collaborative Windows Form Application

Last and certainly least, this is a windows form application that acted as my final for my C# class in the Spring quarter of the 2024-2025 school year
The form acts as catalog for a car dealership, with filters, a comment section, a login screen, and a place to add your own listing. The main thing I helped with was the bugfixing and general formatting for our final submission, so essentially just making it presentable.
